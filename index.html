Therapy

I offer therapy and consultation grounded in research on interpersonal trust, gender dynamics, bias, socio-cultural identity. My approach is intersectionality-informed and socio-politically aware, helping clients understand how their personal and relational experiences are shaped by identity, history, and broader factors, not necessarily under one’s control. Sessions offer a reflective, grounded space to explore relational patterns, understand how past and present experiences shape reactions, and collaboratively develop strategies that feel sustainable and authentic. I support individuals navigating:

  • Personal and interpersonal challenges, including trust ruptures, communication difficulties, relationship strain, attachment patterns, self-doubt, overthinking, and emotional overwhelm
  • Professional and work-related concerns such as identity-based pressures, navigating bias, leadership stress, difficult team dynamics, and cross-cultural uncertainties
  • Life transitions and intrapersonal shifts that require reflecting on one's values, rebuilding confidence, or making sense of competing expectations
